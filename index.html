Autobiography

My name is Kelly P. Canlas, I live in Tondo, Manila. My father's name is Roberto Canlas and my mother's name is Imelda Canlas. I have ten siblings, but two of them have passed away. A short story about myself: I learned early in Tondo, Manila, the importance of perseverance and the strength found in togetherness. I'm studying at the TECHNOLOGICAL UNIVERSITY OF THE PHILIPPINES. I am pursuing my studies to prove to my family that I can graduate and to help them, as I am the only one left studying among my siblings. I'm also trying to achieve despite being very shy because of people's judgments. But I don't need to mind them because I know my own capabilities, and I want to learn more and enhance my knowledge of things I have yet to know or try. Even though I'm sometimes afraid, I manage, especially in my course as a teacher.
